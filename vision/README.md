# 🎯 DealCraft Product Vision & Strategy

Welcome to the **DealCraft Vision & Ideas Hub**. This space is designed to capture, organize, and develop the long-term vision, feature requests, and conceptual ideas for the Negotiation Master simulator.

Since this sits directly in our git repository, all thoughts, plans, and roadmap items are version-controlled alongside our code.

---

## 🚀 The Mission

**DealCraft** aims to be the premier immersive, AI-driven business negotiation simulator. 

Instead of passive reading or generic roleplay, DealCraft provides professionals, students, and teams with an arena to practice realistic, high-stakes negotiations against complex AI personas, backed by real-time coaching from an expert in-game advisor.

## 💎 Core Values & Pillars

1.  **Immersive Realism**: The negotiation simulation should feel like a real boardroom or video call.
2.  **Actionable Coaching**: AI feedback should be constructive, specific, and based on real-world negotiation frameworks (e.g., Harvard Principle, FBI/Never Split the Difference methods).
3.  **Clean & Beautiful UX**: Dark modes, premium glassmorphism, responsive elements, and clean animations to keep learners engaged.
4.  **Replayability**: Dynamic scenarios and randomized AI temperaments make every play session unique.
