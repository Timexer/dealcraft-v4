# 🏆 Product Milestones & Roadmap

This document outlines the development phases of **DealCraft**, mapping its journey from the current MVP to a fully-featured professional training platform.

---

## 🌟 Current Phase: Phase 1 — The Foundation
*   [x] Immersive, fully-responsive web application.
*   [x] Live interactive chat interface with realistic AI opponents.
*   [x] Real-time AI Advisor sidebar providing feedback on every turn.
*   [x] Sleek dark-mode aesthetic with custom animations and glassmorphism.
*   [x] Game-over postmortem analysis showing core strengths and areas of improvement.
*   [x] Integrated Lemon Squeezy tips/donations functionality.
*   [x] Version control, Git-integrated repository, and instant hosting deployments via Vercel.
*   [ ] **Domain Integration**: Connect custom subdomain (`dealcraft.englishbreakfast.pl`) on Vercel and point DNS CNAME record from Bluehost dashboard.

---

## 🚀 Upcoming Phase: Phase 2 — Scenario Depth & Personalities
*   [ ] **Scenario Library**: Expand the simulator to support multiple built-in cases:
    *   *Case A: Salary Negotiation* (High emotion, career growth)
    *   *Case B: B2B Enterprise Agreement* (Procurement tactics, terms & conditions)
    *   *Case C: Vendor Conflict* (Resolving a broken contract / delay)
*   [ ] **The Personality Matrix**: Add distinct AI playstyles (e.g. Shark, Accommodator, Analytical) that alter how easily they accept concessions.
*   [ ] **Advanced Advisor Prompts**: Feed the advisor a structured rubric (like the Harvard Method) to give even deeper, more accurate advice.

---

## 🎙️ Future Phase: Phase 3 — Multimodal & Advanced Feedback
*   [ ] **Speech-to-Text Input**: Allow players to speak their responses. Real-world negotiations happen verbally, making this a critical practice feature.
*   [ ] **Realistic AI Voice Synthesis**: Give the opponent a voice using high-fidelity text-to-speech engines.
*   [ ] **The Negotiation Scorecard**: Interactive metrics dashboard grading the player on Value Captured, Relationship Preserved, and Emotional Control.
*   [ ] **Exportable PDFs**: Professional performance summaries players can download.

---

## 🏢 Scaling Phase: Phase 4 — Platform & Enterprise Ecosystem
*   [ ] **User Accounts (Firebase Auth)**: Allow users to log in, save their history, and track their scores over time.
*   [ ] **Trainer/Teacher Dashboard**: Allow educators or corporate managers to:
    *   Create custom private scenarios.
    *   Invite students/employees to play.
    *   Review their transcripts and grades in one clean dashboard.
